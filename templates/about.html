About

This is a website inspired by GeoGuessr and Wordle, that lets you guess where pieces have been on the chessboard in real chess games from lichess.

This idea for this project came up when hanging out with friends of discord, and so I will attribute the idea for GeoChessr to all of us, mansimum, pixler, philantrop and finally me, kappablanc.

However, this website itself has been fully ideated and developed by me, Yannik Keller.

Big shoutout to lichess, the forever free and open source chess engine that both lets me use its piece designs and has an awesome api that lets me easily grab games for geochessr automatically. I love you lichess!
